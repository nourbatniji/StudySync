What Makes StudySync Different?

StudySync isn't just another study timer. It's a smart warning system that continuously checks whether you're on track for your exams based on your real study habits.

As exam day gets closer, the interface reflects the urgency showing you exactly how many hours you’re behind and what you need to do today to get back on track.

How It Works

1

Add Your Subjects

Enter your exam date, number of chapters, and estimated study time per chapter. StudySync automatically calculates your daily study requirements with precision AI algorithms.

2

Track Your Progress

  • Green: You're on track
  • Yellow: You need to increase your study time
  • Red: Urgent you’re far behind your plan
3

Pomodoro Study Sessions

Use focused 25-minute study blocks with distraction tracking. StudySync logs everything and shows you exactly how much you've studied today with beautiful visualizations.

The Panic Mode System

When you fall behind schedule, panic mode activates. The dashboard background changes to match the urgency level and highlights how many hours you’re missing and how much you should study today.

StudySync recalculates your plan in real time so you always know what to focus on today to stay in control.

The goal: Never wonder if you're prepared. You'll know exactly where you stand.
